# Krosis

## What is Krosis?

**Krosis** means “Sorrow” or “Apologies” in the Dragon Language.

It is an extensive modlist for **Skyrim Special Edition 1.6.1170**, designed to create a more beautiful, atmospheric, and content-rich experience. Krosis preserves the identity of Skyrim while giving its world a darker, more mature tone and substantially expanding both its visuals and gameplay.

The list combines next-generation graphics, rebuilt cities and interiors, modern action combat, hundreds of new animations, deep character progression, major quest expansions, fully voiced followers, and configurable content intended for adult players.

Krosis is built for long playthroughs and exploration. You can fill a museum with artifacts, travel to entirely new lands, experience large questlines, develop relationships with companions, survive in the wilderness, or simply rediscover a dramatically transformed Skyrim.

## Key Features

### Modern Visuals

The visual foundation of Krosis is built around a custom **AMON ENB Reborn — Krosis Cut**, NAT.ENB III, and a comprehensive selection of modern graphical technologies:

* complex parallax and upgraded materials;
* extensive ENB particle lighting;
* Lux, Lux Orbis, and Lux Via;
* enhanced weather, fog, storms, and volumetric lighting;
* overhauled water, vegetation, forests, and landscapes;
* improved architecture, clutter, creatures, and environmental meshes;
* pre-generated DynDOLOD, TexGen, xLODGen, and grass cache.

The result is a highly detailed world that still retains Skyrim’s cold and rugged northern atmosphere.

### A Rebuilt World

Nearly every major city, smaller settlement, and many individual locations have received substantial visual and structural improvements.

The list includes:

* **Cities of the North**;
* **The Great Cities, Towns and Villages**;
* **JK’s Skyrim** and several overhauled city outskirts;
* Grand Solitude;
* Alternative Riften;
* Markarth — City of Stone;
* redesigned versions of Whiterun, Windhelm, and other major cities;
* new walls, bridges, farms, watchtowers, and points of interest.

The world feels denser and more believable without becoming a random collection of decorative objects.

### Detailed Interiors

Inns, shops, palaces, temples, guild headquarters, and player homes have been rebuilt through a large selection of **JK’s Interiors** and complementary interior overhauls.

Locations contain more detail, environmental storytelling, functional spaces, and individual character. Lux lighting gives each interior its own mood, from warm and welcoming taverns to cold ruins and unsettling dungeons.

### Modern Combat

Combat is built around the modern **MCO** framework and supported by:

* Precision;
* SCAR;
* Chocolate Poise;
* Elden Parry;
* Simple Timed Block;
* TK Dodge RE;
* Ultimate NPC Dodging;
* Combat Pathing Revolution;
* new movesets and attack combinations;
* improved enemy behavior;
* dedicated animations for draugr, dragons, and numerous creatures.

Attacks use more accurate collision, while enemies can dodge, block, use abilities, and coordinate more effectively. Core balance can still be adjusted through Simply Balanced and the available MCM menus.

### Character Progression and Magic

The central perk framework is **Vokriinator Black**, combining concepts from several major perk overhauls.

It is supported by:

* Ordinator;

This creates an enormous variety of viable builds, from familiar warriors and mages to highly specialized or hybrid archetypes.

### Enemies and Difficulty

Bandits, undead, vampires, dragons, Dwemer constructs, and other enemies have been extensively expanded.

OBIS, Rogues ’n Raiders, The Restless Dead, Skyrim Revamped, Know Your Enemy 2, Fire and Blood, Savage Skyrim, and related systems introduce new enemy variants, abilities, resistances, combat roles, and unique bosses.

Difficulty remains configurable, but an unprepared adventurer will find Skyrim considerably more dangerous.

### Animations and Controls

Krosis includes a carefully selected animation library covering:

* first- and third-person combat;
* weapons, magic, bows, and crossbows;
* movement, sneaking, and horseback riding;
* swimming, climbing, and traversal;
* contextual and everyday actions;
* NPCs, children, creatures, and undead.

Open Animation Replacer and Pandora provide the modern animation foundation. Preference has been given to animations that support gameplay and atmosphere rather than repeatedly interrupting or distracting the player.

### Survival and Daily Life

SunHelm, Campfire, Stress and Fear, Bathing in Skyrim, Simple Hunting Overhaul, and their supporting mods introduce:

* hunger, thirst, cold, and fatigue;
* cooking and resting;
* camps and wilderness travel;
* hunting and field dressing;
* bathing and dirt;
* psychological stress and methods of relief.

Most of these mechanics can be adjusted or disabled through their MCM menus.

### Characters and Equipment

NPC appearances, body meshes, hair, beards, faces, eyes, scars, and beast-race features have all been overhauled.

The list uses CBBE 3BA and HIMBO, clothing and hair physics, BeastHHBB, Faithful Faces, Realistic RS Children, and numerous individual character replacers.

A wide range of new armor sets, weapons, artifacts, and variations of vanilla equipment are also included. Item and loot distribution is managed through Open World Loot and related systems.

### Adult Content

Krosis is intended for adult players and includes configurable integration of **OStim Standalone**, ORomance Plus, and related addons.

Relationship and intimacy systems are not required for the main game and can be ignored or configured separately. However, the list does contain explicit adult material and should not be considered SFW.

## System Requirements and Performance

Performance testing is still ongoing across different hardware configurations.

On a system equipped with an **Intel Core i7-12700KF, 32 GB of RAM, and an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080**, the list runs at a locked 72 FPS at 1920 × 1080. Drops into the 40–50 FPS range may occur in the heaviest cities, dense forests, and scenes containing large numbers of NPCs.

Significant effort has been made to reduce stuttering and optimize demanding locations. Final performance will still depend on resolution, GPU performance, available VRAM, and selected graphical settings.

## In Summary

Krosis is more than a visual overhaul of Skyrim.

It is a large, mature, and atmospheric adventure that combines modern graphics, a richer world, dynamic combat, deep character progression, new storylines, and memorable companions.

Skyrim remains familiar, but it becomes more beautiful, more dangerous, more sorrowful — and far more expansive.
